Sweet Chilli Chicken

Instagram Recipe
My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

500g Rice

40g Tomato Puree

50g Honey

10ml Worcestershire Sauce

30ml Soy Sauce

40g Sweet Chilli Sauce

2 Red Bell Peppers

600g Chicken Breast

Method

Step 1

Cook the rice.

Step 2

Dice Chicken, cook and set aside.

Step 3

Cook Peppers.

Step 4

Mix the honey, tomato puree, soy sauce, sweet chilli sauce, and worcestershire sauce together with a little bit of water.

Step 5

Add chicken, rice, and sauce to the pan and cook for a few minutes.
