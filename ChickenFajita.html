Chicken Fajitas

BBC Good Food Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

4 Tbsp Lime Juice

4 tsp Cumin

2 tsp Chilli Powder

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

2 tsp Paprika

1 tsp Salt

1 tsp Pepper

3 Bell Peppers

640g Chicken

2 200g bags of Lettuce

50g Sour Cream

8 Wraps

Method

Step 1

Cut chicken into strips.

Step 2

Combine first 7 ingredients together into a marinade.

Step 3

Combine with Chicken for as long as possible.

Step 4

Cut Peppers into thin strips then cook.

Step 5

Remove peppers then cook chicken.
