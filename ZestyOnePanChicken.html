Zesty One Pan Chicken

Slimming World Recipe
My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

1 Lemon

2 Tbsp Capers

640g Chicken Breast

1 400g Can of Chopped Tomatoes

400ml of Chicken Stock

12 Silverskin/Pickled Onions

2 300g Cans of New Potatoes

Method

Step 1

Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of clingfilm. Slightly flatten with a meat hammer.

Step 2

Spray a large non-stick fry pan (non-stick wok also works) with cooking spray and place over high heat. Add the chicken breasts in single layer and fry for 4-5 minutes turning once half way through.

Step 3

Add all the remaining ingredients, season well with salt and pepper and cook for a further 10 minutes. Note: To thicken the sauce, remove the chicken breasts and allowed the remaining ingredients to cook until the sauce thickens which should be about 20 minutes or so.

Step 4

Add the chicken back to the pan and heat through.
