Harissa Lamb and Pepper Couscous

BBC Good Food Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

2 tbsp Harissa Paste

400g Lamb Steaks

3 Red Peppers

200g Mixed Veg

250g Couscous

1 Stock Cube

Method

Step 1

In a large bowl, mix the harissa with the oil, then tip in the lamb, peppers and onions. Add some salt and pepper and toss everything together to coat well.

Step 2

Tip the mixture into a pan and cook.

Step 3

Combine with the couscous prepared as per the instructions with a stock cube.
