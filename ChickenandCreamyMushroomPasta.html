Chicken and Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Slimming World Recipe

Ingredients

500g Pasta

500g Chestnut Mushrooms

1 Bell Pepper

1 400g Can of Chopped Tomatoes

300g Philadelphia

150ml Chicken Stock

1 Tbsp Tomato Puree

640g Chicken Breast

Method

Step 1

Cook the pasta.

Step 2

Cook the Chicken and set aside.

Step 3

Spray a large non-stick frying pan with low-calorie cooking spray and put it over a high heat. Fry the mushrooms for 5 minutes. Add the red pepper and fry for 2 minutes, then add the chopped tomatoes, philadelphia, dried herbs, stock and tomato pur√©e. Bring to the boil, stirring constantly, then turn down the heat and simmer for 6-8 minutes.

Step 4

Stir the mushroom sauce and chicken into the pasta, season lightly and toss to mix well. Scatter over the tarragon and serve in warmed bowls with a salad or vegetables of your choice.
