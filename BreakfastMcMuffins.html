Breakfast McMuffins

Instagram Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

8 Turkey Rashers

4 wholemeal Muffins

120g Mozzrella

6 large eggs

500g 5% Beef Steak Mince

Method

Step 1

Mix 6 eggs into a greased baking dish. Bake at 200 Â°C for 10 minutes. Then cut into 4 even slices.

Step 2

Put mince into a bowl and add paprika, salt, pepper, and garlic. Mix together and seperate into 12 even balls

Step 3

Cook Turkey rashers in the pan.

Step 4

Flatten the patties and cook on the pan.

Step 5

Add each cooked item and mozzarella into a muffin.
