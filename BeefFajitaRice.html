Beef Fajita Rice

My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

500g 5% Beef Steak Mince

250g Rice

160g can of Sweetcorn

160g Can of Peas

2 Bell Peppers

500g Natural Yoghurt

Fajita Mix Seasoning

Method

Step 1

Dice the peppers.

Step 2

Brown the mince with the fajita mix.

Step 3

Add the peppers to the mince and cook until softened.

Step 4

Add the sweetcorn, peas, and rice.

Step 5

Add natural yoghurt to serve.
