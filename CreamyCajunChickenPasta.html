Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta

Instagram Recipe
My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

120g Light Mozzarella

250g pasta

1 red pepper

600g Chicken Breast

300ml Milk

Method

Step 1

Cook pasta.

Step 2

Dice and Cook Chicken. Set aside

Step 3

Cook peppers with garlic until softened.

Step 4

Add milk, mozzarella, and the cooked pasta.

Step 5

Once the mozzarella has melted serve.
