Chicken Orange Chicken

Instagram Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

30g Honey

250g rice

40ml soy sauce

640g Chicken Breast

300ml orange juice

50g cornflour

Method

Step 1

Dice chicken and mix with paprika, salt, pepper, cornflour, and baking powder then cook the chicken on the pan on high heat for 3 minutes on each side until golden and crispy. Set aside.

Step 2

Add garlic, ginger, orange juice, soy sauce, and honey to the pan, bring to a light bubble.

Step 3

Add cornstarch and water to the mix and let it thicken. Once thick, add the chicken back in and coat in the sauce.

Step 4

Add rice to serve.
