Notes

Australian Protein There are 9 amino acids that your body cannot make, and they are known as essential amino acids. The human body can’t store protein and will excrete any excess, so the most effective way of meeting your daily protein requirement is to eat small amounts at every meal. Min. 0.8*kg of bodyweight = protein in grams per day. Preferably min.1g/kg but up to 1.6g/kg is good too. 10%-35% daily calories. More than 2*kg body weight = too much. Medical News Today Article Having Protein about every 3 hours seems to be most optimal as, your muscles continue to produce protein for 2-3 hours after consuming it. around 20-30g each serving is good.
