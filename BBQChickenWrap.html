BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

2 65g Packets of Iceburg Lettuce

50ml Mayocue

200g Turkey Bacon Rashers

600g Chicken Breast

4 wraps (Light Lavish Bread)

Method

Step 1

Dice and cook chicken. set aside and shred with two forks.

Step 2

Fry the turkey bacon then cut into tiny pieces.

Step 3

Use mayocue or bbq sauce and mayonnaise mixed together.

Step 4

Combine the lettuce, chicken, bacon, and sauce together in a wrap.
