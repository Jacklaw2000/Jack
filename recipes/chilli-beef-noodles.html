Chilli Beef Noodles

Instagram Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

300g noodles

8 tsp soy sauce

15g Runny Honey

500g 5% beef mince

400g Stir Fry Veg

Method

Step 1

In a bowl add your mince along with the paprika, garlic salt and onion powder and mix well. In a pan on a medium to high heat add the garlic, ginger and chilli flakes then add the mince mixture and cook until browned.

Step 2

Add stir fry veg to the pan and cook until soft.

Step 3

Add your soy sauce and honey, mix well then let the mince simmer until the liquid disappears and the mince has become crispy. Add your chopped spring onion, noodles and chilli then serve.
