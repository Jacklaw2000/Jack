Beef Stroganoff

Budget Bytes Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

500g Pasta

500g Mince

200g Mushrooms

200g sour cream

2 tsp Dijon Mustard

1 tsp Worcester Sauce

Beef Stock Cube

100g Parmesan

Method

Step 1

Cook pasta according to packet instructions.

Step 2

Cook Mince until Browned.

Step 3

Add Mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes.

Step 4

Add sour cream, Dijon Mustard, Beef stock, and Worcester Sauce. Stir to combine.

Step 5

Add pasta to the sauce and toss together to combine.
