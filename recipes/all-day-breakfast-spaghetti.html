All Day Breakfast Spaghetti

Slimming World Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

250g Spaghetti

3/4 Spring Onions

12 (Turkey) Bacon Rashers

Small Set of Cherry Tomatoes

300g Philadelphia

100g Parmesan

2 tsp Parsley

Method

Step 1

Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling water. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

Meanwhile, spray a large frying pan with fry light and fry the bacon and onions.

Step 3

Fry for 3-4 minutes then add the tomatoes.

Step 4

Fry for another 3 minutes.

Step 5

Add the spaghetti to the bacon mixture and stir well. Add in the philadelphia and parmesan so it coats the spaghetti.

Step 6

Sprinkle with Parsley.
