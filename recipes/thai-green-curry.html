Thai Green Curry

Blue Dragon Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

170ml Thai Green Curry Paste

250g Rice

2 Bell Peppers

600g Chicken Breast

150g Kale

360g Broccoli

400ml Can of Coconut Milk

Method

Step 1

Chop the broccoli into florets, clean the kale and add to a pot of boiling water for 8 minutes.

Step 2

While the veg is cooking, heat the oil in a large saucepan and fry the paste for 4-5 minutes or until the oil separates and the paste smells fragrant.

Step 3

Add the coconut milk and mix with the paste, stirring well.

Step 4

Once the coconut milk is hot, add the chicken and the pepper slices and cook until tender.

Step 5

Mix back in with the rice.
