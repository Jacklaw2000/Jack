Chicken and Chorizo Gnocchi Bake

BBC Good Food Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

60g Chorizo Crumb

500g Gnocchi

640g Chicken Breast

2 Cans of Tomatoes

120g Mozzarella

Method

Step 1

Dice and cook the chicken. Set aside.

Step 2

Heat the oil in a medium pan over a medium heat. Fry the onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft. Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar, and season. Bring to a simmer, then add the gnocchi and cook for 8 mins, stirring often, until soft. Heat the grill to high.

Step 3

Stir the mozzarella and basil through the gnocchi until fully melted.
