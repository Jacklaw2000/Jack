Pasta Bolognese

BBC Good Food Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

120g Light Mozzarella

250g pasta

1 carrot

500g 5% Beef mince

2 cans of tomatoes

Beef stock

onion

Method

Step 1

Cook pasta.

Step 2

Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.

Step 3

Reduce the heat and add the onion, 2 carrots, 2 garlic cloves all finely chopped, then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until it softens.

Step 4

Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince and cook stirring for 3-4 mins until the meat is browned all over.

Step 5

Add 2 tins of tomatoes, the finely chopped leaves from ¾ small pack basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, 2 tbsp tomato purée, 1 beef stock.
