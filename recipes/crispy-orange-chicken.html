Crispy Orange Chicken

Instagram Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

30g Honey

250g Rice

40ml Soy Sauce

640g Chicken Breast

300ml Orange Juice

50g cornflour

Tin of Peas

Tin of Sweetcorn

Method

Step 1

Dice chicken and mix with paprika, salt, pepper, cornflour, and baking powder then cook the chicken on the pan on high heat for 3 minutes on each side until golden and crispy. Set aside.

Step 2

Add garlic, ginger, orange juice, soy sauce, and honey to the pan, bring to a light bubble.

Step 3

Mix cornstarch and water in a mug until the lumps are removed and then add to the mix and let it thicken. Once thick, add the chicken back in and coat in the sauce.

Step 4

Add rice, peas, and sweetcorn to serve.
