Hoisin Turkey Wraps

BBC Good Food Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

1/2 cucumber

50ml Hoisin sauce

600g Turkey breast steak

4 tortillas

Method

Step 1

Heat the grill to high. Mix the turkey or chicken with half of the hoisin sauce so that itâ€™s coated, then spread out onto an ovenproof dish and grill until sizzling. Warm the tortillas under the grill or according to pack instructions.

Step 2

Spread the tortillas with the rest of the hoisin sauce, then use to wrap up the turkey or chicken with the cucumber, onions and watercress. Cut in half and enjoy while still warm.
