Chicken and Broccoli with Noodles

Slimming World Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

250g Noodles

1 Broccoli Head

1 Bell Pepper Sliced

640g Chicken Breast

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

1 tsp Lemon Juice

3/4 Spring Onions

1 tsp Ginger

400ml Chicken Stock

1 Tin of Cocunut Milk

Method

Step 1

Dice Chicken.

Step 2

Add Ginger and Lazy Garlic to pan for 1 minute.

Step 3

Add Stock, Coconut Milk. Cook for a minute of two until mixed thoroughly.

Step 4

Add Salt, Pepper, Lemon Juice. Stir through.

Step 5

Then add Chicken mix through and then add all other ingredients apart from noodles.

Step 6

Turn heat down and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and sauce is thick.

Step 7

Add Noodles after cooking according to package instructions then serve.
