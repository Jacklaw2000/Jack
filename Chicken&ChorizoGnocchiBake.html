Chicken, Chorizo, and Mozzarella Gnocchi Bake

BBC Good Food Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

60g chrorizo crumb

125g Lighter Mozzarella

640g Chicken Breast

500g Gnocchi

2 400g cans of chopped tomatoes

Method

Step 1

Cook Diced Chicken in Pan with spices then set aside.

Step 2

Fry off Chorizo for 5 minutes then tip in tomatoes. Bring to a simmer then add the Gnocchi. Cook for 8 minutes.

Step 3

Add the mozzarella. Once beginning to melt add the cooked chicken.
