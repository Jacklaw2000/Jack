Harissa Beef

Delicious Magazine Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

100g Sweetcorn

300g Carrots & Peas

120g Mozzarella

100g Parmesan

1 can of tomatoes

400g Tin of Tomatoes

20g Harissa Paste

500g 5% Steak Beef Mince

250g Cous Cous

Beef Stock

Method

Step 1

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large saucepan and brown the mince over a medium heat, then add the chopped tomatoes and 150ml cold water. Bring up to the boil, then reduce to a simmer.

Step 2

Cook The cous cous with a stock cube

Step 3

Stir in the harissa, carrots, peas, sweetcorn, and cheese, then season and simmer gently for 20 minutes.
