Pasta with Salmon and Peas

BBC Good Food My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

250g Pasta

1 tsp Butter

2 Spring Onions

1 300g can of Petit Pois

1 Stock Cube

4 Skinless Salmon Fillets

1 300g tub of Creme Fraiche

100g Chives

Method

Step 1

Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the fusilli according to the pack instructions.

Step 2

Meanwhile, heat a knob of butter in a saucepan, once melted add salmon until cooked.

Step 3

Add the peas, crème fraîche and 50ml water. Crumble in the stock cube.

Step 4

Cook for 3-4 mins until cooked through, stir in the chives and some black pepper. Then stir through to coat the pasta. Serve in bowls.
