Crispy Chicken Burger

Instagram Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

10 slices of Danish wholemeal bread

75g of Cornflour

2 large Eggs

640g Chicken Breast

100g mayonnaise

Method

Step 1

Cut the chicken breast in half and use a rolling pin or similar to flatten.

Step 2

Put three slices of bread, toasted in a blender to turn into breadcrumbs with spices like paprika and mixed herbs.

Step 3

Coat chicken in cornflour. Then cover in egg. Then cover with breadcrumb mix.

Step 4

Put chicken in oven for 20 minutes at 200 degrees. Check it is hot in centre before serving.

Step 5

Put chicken between bread with mayo etc.
