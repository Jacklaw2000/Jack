Zesty One Pan Chicken

Slimming World Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

1 Lemon

2 Tbsp Capers

640g Chicken Breast

1 400g Can of Chopped Tomatoes

400ml of Chicken Stock

12 Silverskin/Pickled Onions

2 300g Cans of New Potatoes

Method

Step 1

Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of clingfilm. Slightly flatten with a meat hammer.

Step 2

Spray a large non-stick fry pan (non-stick wok also works) with cooking spray and place over high heat. Add the chicken breasts in a single layer and fry for 4-5 minutes, turning once halfway through.

Step 3

Add all the remaining ingredients, season well with salt and pepper, and cook for a further 10 minutes. Note: To thicken the sauce, remove the chicken breasts and allow the remaining ingredients to cook until the sauce thickens, which should take about 20 minutes or so.

Step 4

Add the chicken back to the pan and heat through.

Note

Can buterfly multiple chicken breasts in the oven and add in after if more than one is required. Chicken can also be diced. It also recommded not to try this recipe again.
