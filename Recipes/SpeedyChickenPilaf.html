Speedy Chicken Pilaf

Good Housekeeping Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

2 x 250g Rice packets

1 Onion

1 Tin of Petit Pois

640g Chicken Breast

100g Spinach

1 Tub Balti Curry Paste

1/2 tsp Cinnamon

2 Tbsp Mango Chutney

Method

Step 1

Heat oil in a large pan and fry onion over medium heat for 5min until beginning to soften. Stir in curry paste and cook for 1min, then add chicken and fry for 8min until cooked through. Add a splash of water if pan looks dry.
