Creamy Sausage Pasta

Creme De La Crumb Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

410g Richmond Thick Pork Sausages

250g pasta

25g Butter

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

400ml Chicken Stock

200g Creme Fraiche

1 tsp Tarragon

100g Parmesan

1 can Peas

1 can Sweetcorn

Method

Step 1

Cook sausages.

Step 2

Cook pasta.

Step 3

Heat butter in pan. Once melted, add garlic.

Step 4

Add chicken stock.

Step 5

Add creme fraiche in slowly in small amounts at a time to avoid clumps.Reduce sauce.

Step 6

Add Parmesan.

Step 7

Add cut-up sausages. Add peas and sweetcorn.

Step 8

Add a small dash of tarragon.

Step 9

Drain pasta. Finally, add pasta and serve.
