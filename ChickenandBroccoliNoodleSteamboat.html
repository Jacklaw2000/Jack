Chicken and Broccoli Noodle Steamboat

Slimming World Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

250g Thin Egg Noodles

1 Broccoli Head

3 Bell Peppers Sliced

640g Chicken Breast

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

1 tsp Chinese 5 Spice

2 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce

1 Lemon Grass Stalk

3/4 Spring Onions

1 tsp Ginger

1.2 Litres Chicken Stock

Method

Step 1

Cook egg noodles according to packet instructions. Drain and rinse under cold running water. Set aside and allow to drain well again.

Step 2

Marinade the chicken in the soy sauce, ginger, garlic and five spice. Set aside.

Step 3

In a large saucepan bring the stock to boiling point on high heat then add the lemon grass stalk, chicken along with the marinade and spring onions. Cook for another 5-6 minutes. Then add the broccoli and peppers, bring to a boiling point again, reduce the heat and cook for another 5-6 minutes.

Step 4

Add the egg noodles to the saucepan. The noodles just need to be heated through.
