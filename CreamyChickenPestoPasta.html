Creamy Chicken Pesto Pasta

My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

150g Kale

360g Broccoli

1/2 jar (100g) of Pesto

600g Chicken Breast

250g Pasta

120g Mozzarella

165g of Philadelphia

Method

Cook broccoli and kale together in a pot.

Step 2

Cook diced chicken breast in pan.

Step 3

Once the broccoli and kale has cooked, remove from pan and use the water to cook the pasta.

Step 4

Once the pasta has cooked. Combine the chicken, pasta, pesto, philadelphia, and mozzarella. Once the mozzarella has melted add the broccoli and kale and serve.
