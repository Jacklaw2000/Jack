Sausage Rolls

My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

62.5g Ready Rolled Puff Pastry

10 Chicken Sausages or similar low calorie option

Method

Step 1

Cut the pastry into approximately 1.5cm wide strips

Step 2

Wrap the strip around the sausage in a helical manner.

Step 3

Place a baking tray and cook as per the sausage instructions. Alternatively use an airfryer.

Step 4

Serve on their own or with a dipping sauce. low calorie tomato sauce is recommended.
