Bacon and Spinach Fusilli

Slimming World Recipe
My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

500g Pasta

400g Spinach

10 (Turkey) Bacon Rashers

200g Ham

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

Method

Step 1

Cook pasta according to packet instructions.

Step 2

Place spinach in a lidded saucepan then pour just enough boiling water to cover the spinach. Cover with lid and leave for 3-5 minutes. After, drain well and squeeze excess water out.

Step 3

Heat a saucepan over medium-high heat. Spray with cooking spray and stir-fry the bacon for 5-6 minutes or until it lightly golden brown.

Step 4

Roughly chopped the spinach then add to the bacon along with the garlic, ham and bacon. Stir-fry for a further 3-4 minutes.

Step 5

Add pasta to the bacon and toss together to combine together.
