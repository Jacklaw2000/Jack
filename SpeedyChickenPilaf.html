Speedy Chicken Pilaf

Good Housekeeping Recipe My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

400g Basmati Rice

1 Onion

640g Chicken Breast

400g Mixed Veg e.g. Broccoli, Carrots etc.

1 Tbsp Curry Powder

1/2 tsp Cinnamon

200g Natural Yoghurt

Method

Step 1

Cook the rice according to the packet.

Step 2

While the rice is cooking. Add the onion and cook for 3 minutes. Then add the chicken and stir fry for 6 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 3

Stir in the vegetables, curry powder, and cinnamon. Stir fry for another 4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Step 4

Drain the rice and add to the chicken stir fry mixture.

Step 5

Serve with Natural Yoghurt.
