High Protein Lasagne

Instagram Recipe

Ingredients

300g Low Fat Cottage Cheese

165g Philadelphia

60ml Milk

2 Carrots

1 can of Tomatoes with Garlic

50g Tomato Paste

1 Beef Stock Cube

9 Wholewheat Lasagne Sheets

120g Mozzarella

5% Beef Steak Mince

Method

Step 1

Add the cottage cheese, philadelphia, milk, herbs, and garlic to a blender.

Step 2

Cook the carrots with the tomato paste fro 5 minutes until soft.

Step 3

Add teh mince to the carrots and cook until browned.

Step 4

Add the beef stock and the can of tomatoes with herbs and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 5

Layer the meat, lasagne sheet, then sauce 3 times in total. Add a covering of mozzarella to the final layer of sauce. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 180Â°C
