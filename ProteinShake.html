Protein Shake

My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

2 30g Scoops Whey

125g Raspberries

150ml Skimmed Milk

1 Banana

5g Flaxseed/Linseed

Method

Step 1

Take clean smoothie cup then place one banana split in two inside.

Step 2

Add Raspberries or Blueberries to the cup.

Step 3

Add the two scoops of Protein powder to the cup.

Step 4

Add the milk to the cup.

Step 5

Firmly seal blender cup and place into blender.

Step 6

Blend and then replace with drinking lid.
