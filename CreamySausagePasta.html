Creamy Sausage Pasta

Instagram Recipe
My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

410g Richmond Thick Pork Sausages

250g pasta

25g Butter

2 tsp Lazy Garlic

400ml Chicken Stock

200g Creme Fraiche

1 tsp Tarragon

100g Parmesan

1 can Peas.

1 can Sweetcorn.

Method

Step 1

Cook sausages.

Step 2

Cook pasta.

Step 3

Heat butter in pan. Once melted add garlic.

Step 4

Add chicken stock.

Step 5

Add creme fraiche.

Step 6

Add cut up sausages. Add peas and sweetcorn.

Step 7

Add tarragon.

Step 8

Drain pasta. Reduce sauce and then add parmesan. Finally add pasta and serve.
