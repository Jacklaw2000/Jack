Creamy Chicken Pasta

Instagram Recipe
My Fitness Pal Macros

Ingredients

120g Light Mozzarella

180g Philadelphia

340ml Water

640g Chicken Breast

20g tomato puree

1 Chicken stock cubes

250g pasta

Method

Step 1

Cook diced chicken breast in pan.

Step 2

Cook Pasta in pot.

Step 3

Mix chicken stock and tomato puree with water.

Step 4

Once the chicken is cooked add chicken stock mixture and pasta.

Step 5

Cook for 10 minutes then add philadelphia and mozzarella.
